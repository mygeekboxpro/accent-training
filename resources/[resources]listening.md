# 🎧 Listening foundation

## Neutral American English

### Focuses on:

1. **Clear pronunciation**
2. **Standard vocabulary**
3. **Minimal regional features**

Used by newscasters, teachers, and most learning materials

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

#### 1. [English as a Second Language Podcast](https://eslpod.com)

    - Slow, clear American speech + explanations
    - 1000+ lessons on real-life topics

#### 2. [BBC Learning English](https://www.bbc.co.uk/learningenglish/)

    - Not American, but very clear and neutral
    - Grammar accuracy
    - Vocabulary clarity

#### 3. [NPR](https://www.npr.org)

    - Clear, professional speech
    - Very neutral American accent
    - Short segments (not overwhelming)
    - Natural conversational phrasing
    - Great for advanced learners.
