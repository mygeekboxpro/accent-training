### 🔊 AI Text-to-Speech (Best for custom sentences)

1. ElevenLabs
    - Very realistic American voices
    - Lets you tweak pacing and emphasis
    - Great for shadowing practice

2. PlayHT
    - Multiple American voices (male/female, regional styles)
    - Good for repeating phrases with consistent rhythm
    - Google Cloud Text-to-Speech
    - Offers different US voices like “en-US-Wavenet”
    - Reliable and clear for pronunciation practice

### 🎯 STRESS AND INTONATION

1. ELSA Speak
    - Gives feedback on stress and pronunciation
    - Highlights which syllables you stress incorrectly

2. Praat
    - Advanced but powerful
    - Visualizes pitch (intonation curves) so you can compare with native audio

### 🧠 RHYTHM & SENTENCE STRESS

1. Forvo
    - Real human recordings (less robotic than TTS)
    - Good for hearing natural stress patterns

2. YouGlish
    - Shows your sentence (or phrase) spoken in real YouTube clips
    - Great for intonation in real context


### 🧩 A simple training routine (very effective)

Take a sentence like:
 > “I didn’t say he stole the money.”

1. Generate audio (ElevenLabs or PlayHT)
2. Notice how meaning changes with stress:
    - I didn’t say… 
    - I didn’t say… 
    - I didn’t say he stole…
3. Repeat each variation aloud
4. Record yourself and compare
